REVISIT

The project

How can we realize a multidisciplinary design, through
optical illusion and interactive circulation, in which
people look for peace and awareness during and after
the corona crisis?

The Project

Revisit, our project, is presented in a rural house in Hombeek, Mechelen. This house was built on a vacant plot of 12 ares and is currently inhabited by the Keupers family. The inhabited house with a homely atmosphere brings our project closer to reality.
The concept around which we wanted to realize our project was very clear from the start, combining an optical illusion with an interactive circulation through lived spaces. In addition, as a group, we found it very important to make positivism an attraction throughout the performance. During the visit, you will travel through time to the good old days that had to take an indefinite break. The idealism of this project will sooner or later be the future of our living worlds.

Ground floor

On the ground floor of the house, a sculpture can be found at the entrance, in the kitchen and in the garden. Furthermore, sound fragments are used in the dining room in combination with a dining table that reflects the presence of company. Video clips play in the living room that emphasize the family atmosphere.

First floor

On the first floor of the house, a sculpture can be found in the play and bedroom. These figures are also enhanced by the other two techniques: audio and image. In the girls' room we applied the technique of sound.
